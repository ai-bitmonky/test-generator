Step 2: Find $g(x)$.

$g(x) = e^x - 2x$

Step 3: Calculate the integral.
$$\int_0^1 f(x)g(x)dx = \int_0^1 2x(e^x - 2x)dx = \int_0^1 2xe^x dx - \int_0^1 4x^2 dx$$
